# Top 10 Wedding Trends for 2025

As we step into 2025, wedding trends are evolving to blend tradition with modern flair. Here’s a curated list of the top 10 trends to make your big day unforgettable.

## 1. Sustainable Decor
Eco-friendly weddings are in vogue. Opt for biodegradable confetti, locally sourced flowers, and reusable decor elements to minimize your environmental footprint.

## 2. Bold Color Palettes
Move over pastels—vibrant jewel tones like emerald, sapphire, and ruby are stealing the show. Mix bold hues with metallic accents for a luxurious vibe.

## 3. Interactive Food Stations
Food is an experience! Think live sushi counters, artisanal cocktail bars, or dessert stations where guests customize their treats.

## 4. Statement Bridal Fashion
Brides are embracing dramatic capes, detachable trains, and non-traditional colors like blush or black for a bold, personalized look.

## 5. Immersive Entertainment
From live painters capturing your ceremony to acrobatic performances, immersive entertainment keeps guests engaged and creates lasting memories.

## 6. Tech-Enhanced Invitations
Digital RSVPs with QR codes and augmented reality invites are making waves, offering a seamless and modern guest experience.

## 7. Micro Weddings
Intimate gatherings with fewer than 50 guests are trending, allowing couples to focus on meaningful connections and curated details.

## 8. Vintage Vibes
Retro-inspired elements like vinyl record guest books or vintage getaway cars are adding nostalgic charm to modern weddings.

## 9. Wellness-Focused Events
Incorporate yoga sessions, mindfulness corners, or healthy catering options to prioritize guest well-being.

## 10. Personalized Ceremonies
Couples are weaving their stories into ceremonies with custom vows, cultural rituals, and unique unity ceremonies like sand blending or tree planting.
