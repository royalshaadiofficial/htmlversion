Our Story

We believe every wedding is a masterpiece waiting to be told. Royal Shaadi is dedicated to the artistry, tradition, and modern elegance of Indian celebrations.

The Vision

Founded in 2026, Royal Shaadi emerged from a desire to bridge the gap between age-old Indian traditions and the contemporary aspirations of today's couples. We curated a space where heritage meets high fashion, and where planning a complex multi-day celebration becomes a journey of joy rather than stress.

What We Do

From our comprehensive Shubh Muhurat calendars to our hand-picked vendor directories, every tool on our platform is designed with the user in mind. Our editorial team works tirelessly to bring you the latest trends in bridal lehengas, décor themes, and lifestyle advice that resonates with the modern Indian identity.

Our Commitment

We are committed to authenticity, inclusivity, and excellence. Whether you're planning a grand destination wedding in Udaipur or an intimate ceremony in your backyard, Royal Shaadi is your trusted companion at every step.
