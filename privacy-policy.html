Privacy Policy

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us for support. This may include your name, email address, and wedding details.

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, to personalize your experience, and to communicate with you about your wedding planning journey.

We use cookies to maintain your session and remember your preferences, such as items in your wedding planner. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party vendors (like venues or photographers) only if you explicitly request us to do so.
